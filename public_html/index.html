Welcome to Coffee & Travel Café

Welcome to Coffee & Travel Café, where we blend the rich flavors of Ethiopian coffee with the spirit of adventure. Our café is a cultural hub where coffee lovers and travelers unite, sharing stories over a cup of perfectly brewed Ethiopian coffee.

As the birthplace of coffee, Ethiopia offers an unparalleled coffee experience. Our café celebrates this heritage while providing expert travel guidance for those wanting to explore this beautiful country.

Our mission is to create a cultural hub where coffee lovers and travelers can connect over premium Ethiopian coffee while discovering the beauty of Ethiopia.

Whether you're a local looking for a cozy spot to enjoy authentic Ethiopian coffee or a traveler seeking guidance for your next journey, we are here to make every experience memorable.
